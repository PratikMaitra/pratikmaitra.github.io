Research Work

Applied ML Tools- I developed the UI for the CAD2Graph Tool under the guidance of Professor Bijaya Adhikari at the University of Iowa, a software tool for automatic conversion of CAD files of healthcare facilities to graphs. The associated research paper was accepted in ECML PKDD 2023.

NLP Application in Nursing/healthcare - I collaborated with Professor Sena Chae at the University of Iowa on applying publicly available BERT based models to a large corpus of clinical notes pertaining to patients suffering from acute myeloid leukemia(AML). We trained and deployed a biobert-based NER model to identify medical symptoms. The research paper was titled Developing a BioBERT-based Natural Language Processing Algorithm for Acute Myeloid Leukemia Symptom Identification from Clinical Notes.

ML Applications - I worked under Professor Subhadip Basu of Jadavpur University on the use of Shapley values for finding a game theoretic approach of the most influential node in a network and implemented the algorithm for the same.

Optimization - I am collaborated with Prof. Wang at the University of Iowa and Yixiang Xu of Berkley as a paid RA for optimization of python code. The initial optimization resulted in a reduction in the run time of processing a 5000-tweet database from 98 minutes to sub-20 minutes.

Noteworthy Projects

Software Design - I have developed an Electronic Medical Representative Software (EMR) as part of my software group project. The software used Express, React, NodeJS, and MySQL for development. The development model followed the Agile Double Iterative Hybrid process and made use of the MERN stack (MySQL, Express, React, Node).

Data Analysis - I have also showcased the various Machine learning Linear regression and Support Vector Machine models on stock price data.

Web Development - I have developed web pages using Google Maps API and Geocode. I have worked with automated scraping tools like Octaparse and Android VMs like Android Studio.
